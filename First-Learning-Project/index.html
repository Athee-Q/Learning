Salah (Prayer)

Muslims are required to perform five daily prayers facing the Kaaba in Mecca. These prayers are performed at specific times:... before dawn, midday, afternoon, after sunset, and at night. Salah involves physical postures, recitation of verses from the Quran, and supplications. >وعن بريدة رضي الله عنه عن النبي صلى الله عليه وسلم : " الذي بيننا وبينهم الصلاة، فمن تركها فقد " (( الترمذي : حديث حسن )). Buraidah (May Allah be pleased with him) reported: Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said, "That which differentiates us from the disbelievers and hypocrites is our performance of Salat. He who abandons it, becomes a disbeliever." [At-Tirmidhi]. Riyad as-Salihin 1079

Zakath(Charity)

Muslims are obligated to give a portion of their wealth (usually 2.5%) to those in need, as a means of purifying their wealth and supporting ... the less fortunate in society. >حَدَّثَنَا ابْنُ نُمَيْرٍ، قَالَ حَدَّثَنِي أَبِي، حَدَّثَنَا إِسْمَاعِيلُ، عَنْ قَيْسٍ، قَالَ قَالَ جَرِيرُ بْنُ عَبْدِ اللَّهِ بَايَعْتُ النَّبِيَّ صلى الله عليه وسلم عَلَى إِقَامِ الصَّلاَةِ، وَإِيتَاءِ الزَّكَاةِ، وَالنُّصْحِ لِكُلِّ مُسْلِمٍ. Narrated Jarir bin `Abdullah: I gave the pledge of allegiance to the Prophet (ﷺ) for offering prayer perfectly, giving Zakat, and giving good advice to every Muslim.

Fasting during Ramadan

Muslims fast from dawn until sunset during the holy month of Ramadan. This fasting includes abstaining ...from food, drink, smoking, and other physical needs.It is a time of spiritual reflection, increased devotion, and self-discipline. حَدَّثَنَا قُتَيْبَةُ، حَدَّثَنَا إِسْمَاعِيلُ بْنُ جَعْفَرٍ، عَنْ أَبِي سُهَيْلٍ، عَنْ أَبِيهِ، عَنْ أَبِي هُرَيْرَةَ ـ رضى الله عنه ـ أَنَّ رَسُولَ اللَّهِ صلى الله عليه وسلم قَالَ " إِذَا جَاءَ رَمَضَانُ فُتِحَتْ أَبْوَابُ الْجَنَّةِ ". Narrated Abu Huraira: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "When Ramadan begins, the gates of Paradise are opened."
